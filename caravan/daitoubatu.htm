The Great Slay

The Great Slay is a cooperative hunt unlocked at HR17 in which you attempt to hunt Raviente,
　※There are two types of Great Slay, one where you hunt with 8-16 players, and one unlocked at HR100 with 24-32 players
Raviente can only be hunted in the Caravan, and there can only be 1 Raviente in a quest.
Each time the party deals a certain amount of Raviente, it returns back to the Caravan to resupply before departing again.
No matter how many times you faint, you won't fail the quest as long as theres a support team to revive you.
The hunt has 3 phases, the Preparation Phase, the Hunt Phase, and the Carving Phase

Preparation Phase
・You can participate in a Great Slay by talking to Kashira in the Pallone Caravan and selecting "Join a Great Slay"
・The Hunting Phase will begin if there are 8 participants (24 participants for Bezerk Raviente)
・If you do not have the required number of people when starting a Great Slay, a 10 minute timer will begin.
　　※If you do not reach the required number of people before it ends, you cannot begin the quest.
WARNING
・Moving to another area or logging out while in a Great Slay party will remove you from the party.
　However, you can still participate in the Carving Phase
　　※Assuming the Great Slay is successful, of course.
・If you participate in a Raviente Hunt in a different Land, your Hunting Phase Contribution Points will be cleared.

Hunting Phase
・In the hunting phase, you must defeat Raviente within 3 hours.
・If the Hunting Morale Gague reaches 0, the quest will fail.
　Depending on progress and damage done to Raviente, itll depleat at a certain rate.
　It recovers to 100% every 4.7 progress (field movement)
　　※Death doesn't have any direct effect, but is undesirable, as you cannot deal damage until you return to the battle.
・If the support team is not in time, the quest will fail, and will be restarted.
・Four NPC's named Busta will accompany you and start to attack Raviente.
　※There are four in BC (Base Camp), but don't do anything.
・During the quest, there is a heat gague that accumulates according to the time elapsed. It increases weapon power and defense as it builds up.
　Weapons with a damage multiplier of less than 240, will be increased up to 240.
　Weapons with a damage multiplier of 240 or higher will be increased to +30.
・If you progress enough or deal a certain amount of damage, you will return to Pallone Caravan.
　You can return twice if your damage outpaces your progression.
